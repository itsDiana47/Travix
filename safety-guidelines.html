Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Follow these simple rules for a secure experience.

For Senders

Before Sending

  • Check traveler's ratings and reviews
  • Describe your item accurately
  • Package items securely
  • Take photos before handover
  • Meet in safe, public places

Never Send These Items

  • Illegal drugs or weapons
  • Dangerous or explosive materials
  • Stolen or counterfeit goods
  • Large amounts of cash

For Travelers

Before Accepting

  • Check sender's profile and ratings
  • Inspect the package carefully
  • Ask what's inside - it's your right
  • Refuse anything suspicious
  • Meet in safe locations

Red Flags - Don't Carry If

  • Sender won't tell you contents
  • Package looks damaged or suspicious
  • Unusual smells or sounds
  • Too much money offered
  • Asked to skip customs checks

During Travel

  • Keep items secure at all times
  • Always declare at customs
  • Update delivery status
  • Get proof of delivery

Payment Safety

  • Only pay through Travix
  • Never use cash
  • Keep all receipts
  • Report suspicious requests

Communication

  • Stay on platform only
  • Be professional
  • Protect your privacy
  • Report harassment
