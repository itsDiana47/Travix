What is Travix?

It sounds cool to earn money while traveling, or save while ordering, right?

Travix makes the dream come true: travel and earn money, or order and save money.

What is Travix?

Travix is a revolutionary peer-to-peer delivery platform that connects people who need to send items with travelers who have extra luggage space. By utilizing journeys that travelers are already making, we create an affordable, fast, and eco-friendly alternative to traditional shipping services.

  • Low Cost: Pay a fraction of standard shipping fees.
  • Verified Community: Profiles, IDs, and reviews for trust.
  • Secure Payments: We hold funds until delivery confirmation.

How Does the System Work?

Our platform enables a secure marketplace where senders and travelers meet. Through our secure system, senders post their delivery needs and travelers accept trips that match their route.

  • Verified Community: ID checks and reviews.
  • Secure Payments: Escrow-based payments with verification.
  • Real-time Tracking: See your item's progress until delivery.
