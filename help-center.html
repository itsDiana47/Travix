Quick answers to common questions

Getting Started

Travix is a peer-to-peer delivery platform that connects people who need to send items with travelers who have extra luggage space. It's a cost-effective and fast alternative to traditional shipping services.

Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner, fill in your details (name, email, password), verify your email address, and complete your profile. You can then start sending items or earning as a traveler!

Travix operates in 156 countries worldwide. When creating a request or trip, you'll be able to see if service is available for your specific route.

Sending Items

You can send documents, electronics, clothing, gifts, cosmetics, books, and more. Prohibited items include weapons, illegal substances, hazardous materials, and perishable goods. Check our full list in the Safety Guidelines.

Pricing is based on weight (starting at $15/kg), item category, and route. Use our Price Calculator to get an instant estimate. You can also negotiate prices with travelers.

Delivery time depends on traveler availability and route. Most deliveries complete within 1-7 days, significantly faster than traditional shipping which can take 2-4 weeks internationally.

Becoming a Traveler

Sign up, complete ID verification, add your upcoming trip details (departure/arrival cities and dates), set your delivery preferences, and you're ready to accept requests and start earning!

Earnings vary by route and items delivered. Average travelers earn $50-$200 per trip. You set your own price per kg and can accept multiple deliveries per trip to maximize earnings.

Pick up items from senders, transport them safely in your luggage, clear customs if required, and deliver to recipients on time. You must also verify item contents and report any issues immediately.

Safety & Security

All travelers undergo ID verification (government-issued ID, selfie verification), background checks, and must maintain a minimum 4.0 rating. We also verify travel documents for each trip.

Payments & Pricing

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, USDT). All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Payment is held securely by Travix until delivery is confirmed. Once the recipient confirms receipt, payment is released to the traveler minus our 15% platform fee.

Travelers receive payment 24 hours after successful delivery confirmation. Funds are transferred to your chosen payment method (bank account, PayPal, etc.).
