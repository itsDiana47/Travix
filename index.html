What Is Travix?

Travix ia a platform that connects people who need to send items with trusted travelers already heading to the same destination. It makes delivery faster, cheaper, and more secure through Verified users and protected payments.

How Travix Works?

For Send For Travel
  1. Create a Request

    Add item details, destination, and preferred delivery time.

  2. Match with a Traveler

    Browse Verifiedtraveler heading to your destination.

  3. Hand Over the Item

    Meet the traveler, verify identity, and give them the package.

  4. Track & Confirm Delivery

    Follow the delivery updates and confirm once item arrives.
