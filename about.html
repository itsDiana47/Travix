Our Mission

Our Vision

A world where geographical boundaries don't limit opportunities, where anyone can send or receive items globally within days, and where travelers turn their journeys into income.

Our Story

How Travix Started

In 2023, our founder was studying abroad and struggled to send important documents home. Traditional shipping was expensive ($200+) and slow (3-4 weeks). A friend traveling home offered to carry the documents for $30, delivering them in just 2 days.

That moment sparked an idea: What if we could connect people who need items delivered with travelers who have extra luggage space?

We built Travix to solve this problem at scale. Today, we've facilitated over 10,000 deliveries across 156 countries, saving senders millions in shipping costs while helping travelers earn extra income.
